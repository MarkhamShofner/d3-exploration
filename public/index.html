Overview

Users can play with visualized data drawn directly from the World Bank.

Each circle represents a country, or a group of countries (e.g. High-Income).

Instructions

Play with dropdown options for the X and Y axes to visualize country indicators.

Hover over individual circles to learn more information.

Units

k - thousand - 10^3

M - million - 10^6

G - billion - 10^9

T - trillion - 10^12

Indicators provided by The World Bank
